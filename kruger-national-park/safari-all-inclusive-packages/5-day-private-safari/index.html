5 Day Kruger National Park All Inclusive Safari

A private, fully guided safari designed for travellers who want a complete Kruger National Park all inclusive safari package experience in a shorter time. Stay inside the park, maximise your time in the bush, and enjoy a flexible safari tailored to your interests.

Ideal for first-time visitors, couples, and anyone looking for excellent Big Five sightings without committing to a longer safari.

Price

From R24,800

Duration

5 Days

Group Size

2–9 Guests

Availability

All Year

5 Day Safari Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and transfer into Kruger National Park, followed by an afternoon game drive en route to camp.

Day 2: Full day exploring the central Kruger region, known for excellent predator sightings.

Day 3: Travel south through the park with game viewing along the way.

Day 4: Full day exploring southern Kruger with flexible game drives.

Day 5: Optional early morning drive and transfer to the airport.

Flexible itinerary • Multiple airport options • Tailored to your travel plans

Why Choose This 5 Day Kruger Safari

This 5 day Kruger National Park all inclusive safari is ideal for travellers who want a complete private safari experience without committing to a longer stay. It gives you enough time to settle into the rhythm of the bush, enjoy meaningful wildlife sightings, and explore both central and southern Kruger at a relaxed pace.

Staying inside the park makes a real difference. It allows us to make the most of the early mornings and late afternoons, when wildlife is often at its most active across Kruger National Park. With a private guide and vehicle, each day can be shaped around your interests, whether you want to focus on predators, photography, or general wildlife in Kruger National Park, or simply enjoying time in nature.

This safari suits couples, families, and first-time visitors especially well. It is long enough to feel immersive, but short enough to fit into a broader South Africa itinerary. If you are looking for a safari that feels personal, flexible, and well paced, this is a strong choice.

What’s Included in This 5 Day Safari

This 5 day Kruger National Park all inclusive safari is designed to be completely seamless. From the moment you arrive, everything is taken care of — allowing you to focus entirely on your time in the bush.

✔ No hidden costs • ✔ Private safari • ✔ Stay inside Kruger National Park

  • 4 nights accommodation inside Kruger National Park

A More Immersive Kruger Safari Experience

This isn’t a rushed, checklist-style safari. Our approach is slower, more flexible, and focused on getting you closer to the real experience of Kruger National Park.

Instead of jumping from sighting to sighting, we take the time to observe behaviour, follow animal movement, and let each moment unfold naturally. Whether it’s watching a pride of lions settle after a hunt, tracking elephants along a riverbed, or simply sitting quietly at a waterhole, the experience becomes far more meaningful.

Staying inside the park allows you to feel fully connected to the environment. Early mornings, late afternoons, and the sounds of the bush at night all become part of your safari — not just the game drives themselves.

With a private guide and a flexible itinerary, each day adapts to you. Some days may be filled with incredible sightings, others may be slower and more relaxed — but that’s what makes a real safari experience.

Designed for travellers who value quality time in the bush over rushing between sightings.

All Inclusive vs Private Safari – Which is Right for You?

5 Day All Inclusive Safari

This option is ideal if you want a completely seamless experience. Everything is taken care of — from accommodation and meals to park fees and daily logistics. It allows you to relax and focus entirely on your time in Kruger National Park.

5 Day Private Safari (Non-Inclusive)

The private safari option offers more flexibility. You still have your own guide and vehicle, but you manage meals and some daily arrangements yourself. This suits travellers who prefer a more independent and customisable experience.

If you want simplicity and a fully guided experience, our all inclusive safari packages are the best choice. If you prefer flexibility and a more hands-on approach, the private safari may suit you better.
