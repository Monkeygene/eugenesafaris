6 Day Kruger National Park All Inclusive Safari

This 6 day Kruger National Park all inclusive safari offers the perfect balance between time, flexibility, and wildlife experience. With your own private guide and vehicle, you’ll explore multiple regions of the park at a relaxed pace, maximising your chances of meaningful sightings.

From the open plains around Satara to the riverine areas of Skukuza and the rugged landscapes near Berg-en-Dal, this safari gives you a well-rounded experience of Kruger National Park. It’s an ideal choice for travellers who want more depth than a shorter safari, without committing to a full week in the bush.

6 Day Safari Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and transfer into Kruger National Park, followed by an afternoon game drive en route to camp.

Day 2: Full day exploring the central Kruger region, known for excellent predator sightings and open savannah landscapes.

Day 3: Continue your safari through the park as you travel south, enjoying game viewing along the way and experiencing a change in habitat.

Day 4: Full day exploring southern Kruger, an area well known for river systems, varied wildlife, and strong Big Five potential.

Day 5: Spend your final full day on safari with flexible game drives tailored to your interests and the movement of wildlife.

Day 6: Optional early morning drive and transfer to the airport.

Flexible itinerary • Multiple airport options • Tailored to your travel plans

Why Choose This 6 Day Kruger Safari

This 6 day Kruger National Park all inclusive safari is ideal for travellers who want a deeper, more rounded safari experience without committing to a full week in the bush. It gives you enough time to explore multiple regions of the park, settle into the rhythm of safari, and enjoy a greater variety of landscapes and wildlife.

Staying inside the park makes a real difference. It allows us to make the most of the early mornings and late afternoons, when wildlife is often at its most active across Kruger National Park. With a private guide and vehicle, each day can be shaped around your interests, whether you want to focus on predators, photography, birding, or general wildlife in Kruger National Park.

This safari suits couples, families, and first-time visitors especially well, but it is also a strong choice for returning travellers who want more time to experience the park properly. If you are looking for a safari that feels personal, flexible, and unhurried, the 6 day option offers one of the best all-round Kruger experiences.

A Deeper, More Immersive Kruger Safari Experience

This isn’t a rushed, checklist-style safari. With six days in Kruger National Park, our approach becomes slower, more flexible, and focused on giving you a deeper connection to the experience.

Instead of jumping from sighting to sighting, we take the time to observe behaviour, follow animal movement, and let each moment unfold naturally. Whether it’s watching a pride of lions settle after a hunt, tracking elephants along a riverbed, or spending time at a productive waterhole, the extra days allow us to stay longer and experience more.

Staying inside the park enhances this even further. Early mornings, late afternoons, and the sounds of the bush at night all become part of your safari — not just the game drives themselves.

With a private guide and a flexible itinerary, each day adapts to you. Some days may be filled with incredible sightings, others may be slower and more relaxed — but over six days, the experience naturally becomes richer and more complete.

Designed for travellers who want more time in the bush and a deeper, more complete Kruger safari experience.

All Inclusive vs Private Safari – Which is Right for You?

6 Day All Inclusive Safari

This option is ideal if you want a completely seamless and well-balanced safari experience. Everything is taken care of — from accommodation and meals to park fees and daily logistics — giving you more time to relax and fully enjoy your safari in Kruger National Park.

6 Day Private Safari (Non-Inclusive)

The private safari option offers more flexibility. You still have your own guide and vehicle, but you manage meals and some daily arrangements yourself. This suits travellers who prefer a more independent and customisable approach while still benefiting from expert guiding.

If you’re looking for a fully guided, stress-free experience, our all inclusive safari packages are the best choice. If you prefer more flexibility and independence, the private safari may suit you better.
