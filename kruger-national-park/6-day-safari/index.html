6-Day Kruger National Park Safari: All-Inclusive vs. Flexible Itineraries

This 6-day Kruger National Park safari offers the most complete, immersive experience inside the gates. With five nights spent directly within legendary SANParks rest camps, you will explore multiple distinct wildlife regions at a relaxed pace, maximizing your time on active game drives when the bush comes alive.

We offer this flagship itinerary in two distinct tiers to fit your travel style. Choose our Fully Hosted & Catered Safari for a seamless experience where your private guide handles all meals, camp logistics, and conservation fees. Or, opt for our Flexi-Safari—giving you the same expert guiding and rest camp accommodations, while leaving your meals and park fees open to your own schedule and budget.

100% Flexible Flight & Route Logistics

Because we prioritize optimizing your time in the bush, this traverse safari can be executed comfortably in either direction. Depending on your airline preferences and ticket availability, you can choose to land at Eastgate Airport (Hoedspruit) and depart via Skukuza Airport, or run the entire sequence perfectly in reverse. Your guide coordinates all regional airport transfers seamlessly.

Your 7-Day Bush Experience: The Itinerary

No matter which level of hosting you choose, you will share the exact same incredible routes, wildlife viewing tracks, private safaris and classic rest camp locations with your professional guide.

Camps 1 & 2 • Satara (2 Nights)

Days 1 - 2: Orpen Gate Entry & The Plains of Satara

Your safari begins with your arrival at Eastgate Airport (Hoedspruit). After meeting your guide, we make the short drive to Orpen Gate to enter the park. The route toward Satara immediately introduces you to open savannah landscapes—renowned as the predator capital of Kruger due to the high density of lion prides, cheetahs, and hyenas that hunt in these wide-open spaces.

Over these two days, we will embark on early morning and late afternoon game drives along the famous S100 and Nwanetsi river roads, maximizing our chances of witnessing active big cat behavior.

Fully Hosted Tier

Your guide prepares classic bush breakfasts at picnic spots and handles traditional wood-fired braais under the stars back at the Satara communal cooking bomas.

Flexi-Safari Tier

Enjoy evening flexibility. Cook your own dinner on your bungalow’s private braai grid, or dine at the on-site Satara restaurant after your game drive.

Camps 3 & 4 • Skukuza (2 Nights)

Days 3 - 4: Down the Spine of Kruger to Skukuza

On Day 3, we pack up early and execute a slow, game-drive traverse heading south. As we move closer to the Sabie River system, the vegetation thickens into dense riverine woodlands—prime territory for the elusive leopard and massive herds of elephants.

Our home base for the next two nights is Skukuza Rest Camp, the historic heart of Kruger. From here, we patrol loops along the riverbanks where hippos wallow and crocodiles bask, providing fantastic visual framing for wildlife photography.

Fully Hosted Tier

All meals are seamlessly provided. Enjoy cooked meals along the Skukuza boardwalk overlooking the Sabie River, managed entirely by your guide.

Flexi-Safari Tier

Take advantage of Skukuza’s extensive infrastructure. You can visit the Cattle Baron restaurant, grab snacks at the large park shop, or self-cater at your leisure.
