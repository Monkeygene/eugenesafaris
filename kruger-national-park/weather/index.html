Kruger National Park Weather

Understanding the Kruger National Park Weather is essential for planning a successful safari. Whether you're visiting in the green summer or dry winter, weather plays a major role in shaping your wildlife encounters, comfort, and overall safari experience. This guide explores the annual climate patterns in Kruger National Park, helping you decide when to go and what to expect.

Kruger National Park has a subtropical climate. Summers, from November to March, are hot, humid, and wet.
