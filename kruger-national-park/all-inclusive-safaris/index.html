Kruger National Park Safari All Inclusive Packages

Looking for a genuine Kruger National Park safari without the stress of planning every detail yourself?

Our Kruger National Park safari all inclusive packages include accommodation inside the park, conservation fees, airport transfers, daily game drives, traditional South African meals and bottled drinking water throughout your safari. From the moment you arrive until the day you depart, everything is arranged so you can focus on enjoying the wildlife and the experience of being in Kruger.

Unlike many safari operators that stay in a single area, our safaris are designed to explore multiple regions of the park. By travelling between camps such as Satara, Skukuza and Berg-en-Dal, we experience different habitats, landscapes and wildlife areas while giving you a broader understanding of the landscapes, wildlife and ecosystems that make Kruger National Park so special.

At Eugene Safaris, we believe a safari should not feel rushed. While the Big Five are always a focus, we do not race around the park chasing sightings. Instead, we take our time, enjoy the rhythm of the bush and allow nature to unfold naturally. Some of the most memorable moments on safari are often the ones you never expected.

If this is your first time visiting South Africa or your are returning to Kruger once again, our all inclusive safari packages offer a comfortable, authentic and fully guided way to experience the park.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a Kruger National Park all inclusive safari packages? ⌄

Our all inclusive safaris include accommodation inside Kruger National Park, all meals, game drives, conservation fees, and a professional guide. We also handle transfers and logistics.

How many days should I spend in Kruger National Park? ⌄

We recommend at least 5 days for the best wildlife experience. Longer safaris increase your chances of seeing the Big Five and allow you to explore different regions of the park.

Do you stay inside Kruger National Park? ⌄

Yes, all our safaris stay inside Kruger National Park rest camps, allowing early morning and late afternoon game drives for optimal wildlife viewing.

Are your safaris private? ⌄

Yes, our safaris are fully private. You will have your own guide, vehicle, and flexible schedule tailored to your interests.

When is the best time to visit Kruger National Park? ⌄

The dry winter months from May to September offer excellent wildlife viewing, but Kruger is a year-round destination with great birding and lush landscapes in summer.
