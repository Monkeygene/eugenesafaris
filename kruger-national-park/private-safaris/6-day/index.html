6 Day Kruger National Park Private Safari

Enjoy a perfectly balanced Kruger National Park safari with your own private guide and vehicle. This 6 day private safari gives you the ideal amount of time to explore multiple regions of the park at a relaxed pace.

Availability

Why Choose This 6 Day Private Safari

This 6 day private safari offers the perfect balance between time and flexibility. It gives you enough time to explore multiple regions of Kruger National Park without feeling rushed, while still fitting comfortably into most travel itineraries.

With your own guide and vehicle, each day can be shaped around your interests. Whether you want to focus on predators, photography, birding, or simply enjoying time in nature, the pace is entirely up to you.

Staying inside Kruger National Park allows us to maximise early mornings and late afternoons, when wildlife in Kruger National Park is most active.

6 Day Safari Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and transfer into Kruger National Park, with an afternoon game drive en route to camp.

Day 2: Full day exploring the central Kruger region, well known for predator activity and open savanna.

Day 3: Continue exploring central Kruger with flexible game drives and time at productive waterholes.

Day 4: Travel south through the park with game viewing along the way.

Day 5: Full day exploring southern Kruger with flexible game drives.

Day 6: Optional early morning drive and transfer to the airport.

Flexible itinerary • Covers multiple regions • Tailored to your travel plans

A Balanced Kruger Safari Experience

This safari strikes the ideal balance between time in the bush and flexibility. With six days in the park, you can explore more areas while still having time to slow down and enjoy each moment.

Some days will be filled with exciting sightings, while others may be slower and more relaxed. This natural rhythm is part of what makes a real safari experience.

With a private guide and vehicle, your safari becomes something personal — shaped by your interests, not a fixed schedule.

Private Safari vs All Inclusive

A private safari gives you flexibility and control, while our 6 day all inclusive safari offers a fully seamless experience with meals and logistics included.
