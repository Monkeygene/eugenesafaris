5 Day Kruger National Park Private Safari

Experience a flexible and immersive Kruger National Park safari with your own private guide and vehicle. This 5 day private safari is ideal for travellers who want a personalised experience, with the freedom to explore the park at their own pace.

Price

From R18,800

Duration

5 Days

Group Size

2–9 Guests

Availability

Year Round

Why Choose This 5 Day Private Safari

This 5 day private safari is perfect if you want a complete Kruger experience without committing to a longer stay. It gives you enough time to settle into the rhythm of the bush, enjoy meaningful wildlife sightings, and explore different regions of the park at a relaxed pace.

With your own guide and vehicle, every day is flexible. You can spend more time at sightings, focus on specific animals, or simply enjoy being in nature without feeling rushed.

Staying inside Kruger National Park allows us to make the most of early mornings and late afternoons, when wildlife in Kruger National Park is most active.

5 Day Safari Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and transfer into Kruger National Park, followed by an afternoon game drive en route to camp.

Day 2: Full day exploring the central Kruger region, known for excellent predator sightings.

Day 3: Travel south through the park with game viewing along the way.

Day 4: Full day exploring southern Kruger with flexible game drives.

Day 5: Optional early morning drive and transfer to the airport.

Flexible itinerary • Multiple airport options • Tailored to your travel plans

What’s Included in This 5 Day Safari

This 5 day private safari includes everything you need for a flexible and comfortable experience, while giving you the freedom to choose your own meals and pace.

Included

  • Private guided game drives
  • Accommodation inside Kruger National Park
  • Airport transfers
  • Flexible itinerary

Not Included

  • Meals and drinks
  • Conservation fees
  • Flights and travel insurance

A More Flexible Safari Experience

This isn’t a rushed safari. With a private setup, you can take your time, follow animal behaviour, and enjoy each moment without pressure.

Some days may be filled with incredible sightings, while others are slower and more relaxed. That’s what makes a real safari experience — not just ticking off animals, but being present in the environment.

With the freedom to shape each day, this safari becomes something personal — not just a schedule to follow.

Private Safari vs All Inclusive

A private safari gives you flexibility and control over your experience, while our all inclusive safari offers a fully managed, seamless option.
