Kruger National Park a Complete Safari Guide

A Kruger National Park private safari gives you the freedom to experience Africa's most famous wildlife destination on your own terms. With your own dedicated guide and private safari vehicle, there's no need to follow another group's schedule, rush through sightings, or compromise on what interests you most. Whether you're hoping to spend time with a leopard, focus on birdlife, or simply enjoy the bush at a slower pace, every safari is shaped around you.

Every Kruger National Park private safari we run is shaped entirely around the people in the vehicle. How long you sit with a leopard in a marula tree. Which roads you skip because the morning's already given you what you came for. Whether today turns into a birding day because the rollers are putting on a show. The park doesn't change for anyone. How you get to experience it does.
