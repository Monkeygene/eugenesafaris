When to Visit Kruger National Park

Our Kruger National Park All Inclusive Packages run year-round. Each season offers different weather in Kruger and something unique:

Dry Season (May to September)

Cooler weather, sparse vegetation, and better wildlife visibility. Ideal for Big 5 sightings and photography.

  • Sparse vegetation and cooler temperatures
  • Excellent Big Five viewing and photography

Green Season (October to April)

Lush landscapes, baby animals, and over 500 bird species including summer migrants. Perfect for photographers and birdwatchers.

  • Lush greenery and dramatic skies
  • Baby animals and bird migration season
