5-Day Kruger National Park Safari Our Most Complete Big 5 Safari Experience

Unlike shorter safaris that focus on a single region, this 7-day Kruger National Park safari gives you time to slow down, explore multiple wildlife habitats, and revisit productive areas when sightings are at their best. By spending a full week inside the park, you maximise your opportunities to see the Big 5, enjoy exceptional birdlife, and experience Kruger at a relaxed pace without feeling rushed from one destination to the next.
