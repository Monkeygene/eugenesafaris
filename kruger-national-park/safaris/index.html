We offer a range of private Kruger National Park safari packages to suit different travel styles and budgets. Whether you're looking for the convenience of an All Inclusive Safari, the flexibility of a Standard Safari, or the focus of a Photographic Safari, each option guarantees a fully private experience, no shared vehicles, just you and your guide. While our All Inclusive packages cover everything from meals to park fees, our Standard safaris give you the freedom to customize certain elements.
