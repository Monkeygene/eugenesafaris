Kruger National Park Safari Packages

We offer a range of private Kruger National Park safari packages to suit different travel styles and budgets. Whether you're looking for the convenience of an All Inclusive Safari, the flexibility of a Standard Safari, or the focus of a Photographic Safari, each option guarantees a fully private experience—no shared vehicles, just you and your guide. While our All Inclusive packages cover everything from meals to park fees, our Standard safaris give you the freedom to customize certain elements. Explore all three options below and find the perfect fit for your Kruger adventure.

Classic Kruger Safaris

Experience classic safaris in the heart of Kruger National Park. Enjoy private game drives with expert guides, exploring diverse ecosystems and abundant wildlife.

All-Inclusive Kruger Safaris

All-Inclusive safaris offer a hassle-free experience with everything taken care of. Enjoy private accommodations, meals, and guided tours, allowing you to focus on the adventure.

Photographic Safaris

Photographic Safaris in Kruger National Park are designed for photography enthusiasts. Capture stunning wildlife moments with expert guides who know the best spots and times for photography.

Why We Recommend Longer Kruger National Park Safaris

While 3-day Kruger National Park safaris remain popular for travelers short on time, they often only allow for one full day in the bush. We believe the magic of a true Kruger experience unfolds over longer stays — ideally 4 nights or more. With extra time, you ease into the rhythm of the wild, enjoy deeper wildlife encounters, and create lasting memories without the rush. Learn why we recommend longer Kruger safaris.
