Birds of Kruger National Park - Complete Species List

Kruger National Park is one of the greatest birding destinations in Africa, with over 500 recorded bird species. The park’s diverse habitats, including rivers, woodlands, grasslands, and wetlands, support an extraordinary variety of birds ranging from massive eagles to tiny sunbirds.
Birding in Kruger National Park is rewarding throughout the year, with excellent sightings of iconic species such as the Lilac-breasted Roller, Saddle-billed Stork, Southern Ground Hornbill, and numerous birds of prey. Summer brings migratory species, while winter offers excellent visibility and easier spotting.
Whether you are a beginner or experienced birder, Kruger National Park offers exceptional birdwatching opportunities on every drive.
