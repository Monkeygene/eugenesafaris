When to Visit Kruger National Park

Kruger is incredible all year round, and each season offers something special. We'll help you decide when to travel based on what you want to see most.

Dry Season (May to September)

This is the classic safari season. With sparse vegetation and animals gathering at waterholes, it's perfect for Big Five sightings and predator action. Cool mornings and golden evening light also make for amazing photos.

Green Season (October to April)

After the rains, the park transforms into a lush paradise alive with birdsong, babies, and blooming flowers. It's ideal for birders, photographers, and travelers who love nature's vibrant, dramatic beauty — plus, the camps are quieter and rates more affordable.
