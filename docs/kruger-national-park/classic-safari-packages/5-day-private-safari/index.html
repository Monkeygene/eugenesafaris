5-Day Classic Private Safari in Kruger National Park

Experience the legendary wildlife of Kruger National Park on our immersive 7-day Kruger National Park All Inclusive Safari. Your journey begins at Eastgate (Hoedspruit) Airport, from where you'll enter Kruger Park through Orpen Gate and head into the bush in a safari vehicle. Over the next six nights you'll stay at three iconic rest camps - Satara (2 nights), Skukuza (2 nights), and Berg-en-Dal (2 nights) - experiencing diverse landscapes and wildlife at every turn. Each day offers flexible private game drives (morning or full-day) led by an expert guide; with a private safari you set the pace, choosing how long to observe lions, elephants, rhinos, giraffes and other Big Five game. Enjoy authentic South African hospitality at camp each evening and delightful meals under the African sky.

Day 1: Arrival at Eastgate Airport

Arrive at Eastgate (Hoedspruit) Airport, meet your guide and transfer into Kruger Park via Orpen Gate. Enter the park in time for an afternoon game drive towards Satara Rest Camp. Settle into your chalet and enjoy dinner at camp.

Accommodation: Comfortable Bungalow in Satara Rest Camp

Day 2: Explore the area around Satara

Full day exploring Satara's open savannah, famous for its big cats. Begin with an early morning game drive (when predators are most active) through grasslands often called “Lion Central”. Return mid-day for brunch at camp, then head out again in the afternoon. Evening at leisure in Satara.

Accommodation: Same as Day 1.

Day 3: Game Drive down to Southern Kruger

After a sunrise drive and light breakfast, depart Satara and head south toward Skukuza Rest Camp. Lunch will be en route or upon arrival at Skukuza. This camp, the park's largest, sits in the heart of Big Five country along the Sabie River. Enjoy an afternoon drive in this area or relax at camp's pool or viewing decks before dinner.

Accommodation: Bungalow in either Lower Sabie or Skukuza Rest Camp

Day 4: Explore Southern Kruger

A full day based at Skukuza to spot wildlife along the shady riverine areas and open plains. Your guide will tailor the day to your interests - whether tracking elephants by the river or searching for leopards in the forest. Brunch is served back at camp mid-day. In the evening you might join an optional nocturnal game drive or simply stargaze around a campfire.

Accommodation: Same as Day 3

Day 5: Departure to Skukuza Airport

Optionally rise for one last early game drive at dawn (Depending on your flight time). After a light breakfast (depending on your flight schedule), say farewell to Kruger as you drive to Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport for your departing flight. (Breakfast included if time allows; brunch and dinner not included.)

Day 1: Arrival at Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport (KMIA)

Arrive at KMIA, meet your guide, and enter Kruger National Park via the Numbi or Paul Kruger Gate. Enjoy your first afternoon game drive en route to Skukuza or Lower Sabie Rest Camp. Settle into your chalet and relax with dinner at camp.

Accommodation: Bungalow in Skukuza or Lower Sabie Rest Camp

Day 2: Explore Southern Kruger

Spend a full day discovering the southern region's riverine forests and open savannah. Early morning and afternoon game drives offer chances to see the Big Five, especially leopards and elephants along the Sabie River. Brunch is served at camp midday, with time to relax at the pool or on the deck before your evening drive.

Accommodation: Same as Day 1.

Day 3: Transfer to Central Kruger (Satara)

After a sunrise game drive and light breakfast, depart for Satara Rest Camp, located in the heart of Kruger's big cat country. Game drive en route and arrive in time for lunch at camp. Spend the afternoon exploring Satara's iconic grasslands before dinner and a relaxing evening.

Accommodation: Bungalow at Satara Rest Camp

Day 4: Explore the Savannas of Satara

Full day of game drives in Satara's open plains—prime habitat for lions, cheetahs, and other predators. Customize your day with morning and afternoon drives, or even a full-day excursion with picnic stops in the bush.

Accommodation: Same as Day 3

Day 7: Departure to Eastgate (Hoedspruit) Airport

Optional final sunrise drive. After a light breakfast (depending on your flight time), transfer to Eastgate (Hoedspruit) Airport for your outbound flight.

Day 1: Arrival at Skukuza Airport

Arrive at Skukuza Airport, meet your guide, and transfer into Kruger National Park. Settle into your bungalow at Skukuza Rest Camp and enjoy your first afternoon game drive along the Sabie River, known for frequent sightings of elephants, leopards, and hippos. Return to camp for dinner under the stars.

Accommodation: Comfortable Bungalow in Satara Rest Camp

Day 2: Explore Southern Kruger

A full day of game viewing in the southern section of the park, exploring riverine forests and open plains. Morning and afternoon drives are tailored to your interests, with a break for brunch back at camp. Relax at the camp pool or take in the views from the Sabie River deck in between.

Accommodation: Same as Day 1.

Day 3: Transfer to Satara via Central Kruger

After an early game drive and light breakfast, depart Skukuza and journey north toward Satara Rest Camp, game viewing all the way. Arrive at Satara in time for lunch and enjoy an afternoon safari in one of the best areas in the park for predators—especially lions and cheetahs.

Accommodation: Bungalow at Satara Rest Camp

Day 4: Safari in the Open Grasslands of Satara

Spend a full day exploring Satara's wide open plains, often referred to as “Big Cat Country.” Head out early to track lions or hyenas on the move, return to camp for brunch, and then enjoy a relaxing afternoon before an evening game drive.

Accommodation: Same as Day 3

Day 7: Departure from Skukuza Airport

Optionally enjoy one last early morning game drive. After a light breakfast (if time allows), transfer to Skukuza Airport for your departing flight.

All itineraries are flexible and can be adjusted to suit your travel plans. We can start at any airport and end at another — for example, you could begin your adventure at KMIA and fly out from Skukuza Airport.
