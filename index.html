Why Choose a Private Kruger Safari

A private Kruger National Park safari gives you something that's increasingly difficult to find in modern travel: complete flexibility. Instead of sharing a vehicle with strangers, following a fixed schedule, or rushing between sightings, the day unfolds around your interests and priorities.

Whether you'd like to spend an extra twenty minutes watching a leopard in a marula tree, focus on photographing birds along a river, or simply enjoy a quieter pace through the bush, a private safari allows us to adapt the experience as the day develops.

With Eugene as your dedicated guide, you'll benefit from years of experience interpreting animal behaviour, reading alarm calls, and understanding how wildlife moves through different habitats. Rather than trying to satisfy a vehicle full of guests, every decision is made with your safari experience in mind.

Most guests choose one of our all-inclusive safari packages, that's the difference between seeing Kruger National Park and truly experiencing it.

The Wildlife Experience

A private safari isn't just about having your own vehicle. It's about having the freedom to experience Kruger National Park at a pace that allows wildlife encounters to unfold naturally.

We don't measure a safari by how many kilometres we can drive or how many sightings we can tick off a list. Some of the most memorable moments in Kruger happen when you slow down — staying with a pride of lions a little longer, watching elephants interact around a waterhole, or listening to alarm calls reveal a predator hidden nearby.

With no shared schedule to follow, every day can adapt to the wildlife, the conditions, and your interests. That's what makes a private safari such a rewarding way to experience Kruger National Park.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the most common questions about our Kruger National Park private safaris.

What makes a Kruger National Park private safari different? +

A private safari means the vehicle is reserved exclusively for you and your travelling companions. There are no shared game drives, fixed group schedules, or compromises on what wildlife interests you most.

Will we share the safari vehicle with other guests? +

No. Every safari is conducted as a private safari for your booking only. You'll have your own guide and vehicle throughout the experience.

What wildlife can I expect to see? +

Kruger National Park is home to the Big Five, cheetah, African wild dog, giraffe, zebra, hundreds of bird species, and much more. While wildlife sightings can never be guaranteed, our itineraries focus on areas known for excellent game viewing.

Is Kruger National Park safe to visit? +

Yes. Kruger National Park is one of Africa's most established safari destinations and welcomes visitors from around the world every year. We also assist with planning, transfers, and logistics to help make your journey as smooth as possible.

Are airport transfers included? +

Airport transfers are included on our all-inclusive safari packages. Full details can be found on the individual package pages.

Can the safari be customised? +

Absolutely. While most guests choose one of our all-inclusive safari packages, private safaris can be tailored around birding, photography, specific wildlife interests, or preferred trip durations.
