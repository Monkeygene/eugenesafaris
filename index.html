Experience Kruger National Park on a private safari or choose a fully all-inclusive experience designed entirely around you. No rushed schedules, no crowded vehicles, just a calm, immersive journey through one of Africa’s greatest wildlife destinations.

Why Experience Kruger with Eugene Safaris?

A safari in Kruger National Park is something that stays with you long after you leave. It is one of Africa’s great safari destinations, ideal for first-time visitors and seasoned safari travellers alike. Our private safaris and all inclusive safaris are designed entirely around you, giving you the freedom to explore at your own pace and focus on the moments that matter most.

Private Safaris Only

No shared vehicles and no rigid group schedules. Just you, your guide, and the chance to experience Kruger’s wildlife in a more personal and relaxed way.

Expert-Led Experiences

With Eugene Booysen’s local knowledge and guiding experience, each drive becomes more meaningful. From better positioning at sightings to understanding animal behaviour and the smaller details of the bush, the experience goes far beyond simply ticking species off a list.

Slow Safari Style

More time at sightings, less rushing between locations, and a deeper connection to the landscape. This is a safari style built around immersion, patience, and truly appreciating the rhythm of the bush.

All-Inclusive Options

From carefully chosen rest camps to meals and seamless logistics, the details are taken care of for you. That means you can focus fully on enjoying your safari without the stress of planning every step.

You are not only coming to see the Big Five. You are coming to slow down, take in the beauty of the Lowveld, and experience Kruger National Park in a way that feels personal, unhurried, and unforgettable.
