Birding Safaris in Africa

I offer three Kruger National Park Safari all inclusive packages you get to browse, from four to seven days. The more time you spend on safari in Kruger National Park the more you get to witness. My safaris are all inclusive upon arriving at the gate of the national park, traveling to the park etc are not included in these packages.

My Kruger National Park Safari all inclusive packages are designed to give you maximum wildlife viewing, expert guidance and comfort without the hassle of planning every detail yourself. I enjoy taking it slow, so you get to see as much as you possibly can.
